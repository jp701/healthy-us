Name: Ascoril LS Syrup
Price: 95 rupees
Manufacturer: Glenmark Pharmaceuticals Ltd
Use: Cough with mucus
Description:
Ascoril LS Syrup is a combination medicine used in the treatment of cough with mucus. Ascoril LS Syrup is taken with or without food in a dose and duration as advised by the doctor. You should keep taking this medicine for as long as your doctor recommends. If you stop treatment too early your symptoms may come back and your condition may worsen.
